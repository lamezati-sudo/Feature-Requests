Zero Tolerance for CSAE

Hooly AI: Rizz Assistant and its developer, Leonel Mezatio, maintain an absolute zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form. Prohibited Content & Behavior

The following are strictly forbidden on Hooly AI: Rizz Assistant

  • Any content that sexually exploits, abuses, or endangers minors in any form
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) — generating, sharing, storing, or distributing
  • Using the app to groom, solicit, or communicate inappropriately with minors
  • Any AI-generated content that sexualizes or endangers children
  • Attempts to use the keyboard or AI features to produce child-harmful content
  • Sharing personal information of minors without parental consent

How We Protect Children

Active measures taken to ensure child safety

  • AI features are designed and monitored to prevent generation of harmful content
  • User reports are reviewed promptly and violations result in immediate action
  • Accounts found violating child safety policies are permanently banned
  • All confirmed CSAM is reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement in child safety investigations
  • Regular review of app features to identify and mitigate child safety risks

How to Report a Concern

Multiple channels to report child safety issues

1

In-App Reporting

Open the app menu → tap Report Issue → describe the child safety concern. This reaches our team directly.

2

Email Our Child Safety Contact

For urgent child safety matters, email us directly. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

3

Report to Authorities

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact local law enforcement or report to the NCMEC CyberTipline.
